Bearded dragons are the best pets. I love mine very much. His name is nubz

What food does he eat?

Nubs eats collard greens but he really loves bell peppers

Here is some basic information about bearded dragon diets from Petsmart.com

Insects: crickets, mealworms and wax worms, dust with calcium supplement twice weekly Vegetables: chopped dark leafy greens or carrots fed every other day = 25-50 percent of diet, depending upon age Fruits: apples, bananas, melons once a week = 5 percent of diet Prepared diets: pelleted blend once daily Water: Change daily and provided at all times!
