My Favorite Animals!

Animal Gallery

I've put together a gallery of some photos and videos of some of my favorite animals. I hope you enjoy!

Aregentine Black and White Tegu

Number one on my favorite animals list is the Aregentine Black and White Tegu! They are the largest of tegu lizards, and have beautiful markings. They also make great pets!

Tapir

Number two on my favorite animals list is the Tapir!They live in South America and have a prehensile nose trunk. Unfortunately there are less than 3,000 left in the wild.

Sumatran Rhino

Number three on my favorite animals list is the Sumatran Rhino! They live in only live in Indonesia but used to live as far as India and the Himilayas. Unfortunatly there are less than 80 left in existence due to loss of habitat and illegal wildlife trade.
